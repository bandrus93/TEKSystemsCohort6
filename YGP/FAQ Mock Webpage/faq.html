  • Who can place an Adult-Use delivery order?
  • Anyone who is 21+ years of age, lives within the delivery radius, and has completed the ID verification step.
  • Who can accept an Adult-Use delivery order?
  • Only the ordering customer can accept a delivery order. A delivery driver will validate that the customer's physical ID matches the encrypted ID on file.
  • How do I order Adult-Use delivery?
  • Once you have an account and your ID has been verified, you can order directly from any of our partnered dispensaries.
  • Where do you deliver?
  • We deliver across the Greater Boston, Northampton and Pioneer Valley areas. You can input your address in the account section to verify your delivery address.
